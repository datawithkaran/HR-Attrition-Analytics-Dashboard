# 📊 HR Workforce & Attrition Analytics Dashboard

> End-to-end HR analytics project on 1,470 employees to identify workforce trends, employee distribution patterns, and the key factors contributing to employee attrition.

![Dashboard](HR_Dashboard_Screenshot.png)

---

## 🧩 Problem Statement

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges faced by organizations, leading to increased recruitment costs, productivity loss, and workforce instability.

Key questions:

* What is the overall attrition rate?
* Which departments and job roles have the highest employee concentration?
* Does overtime contribute to employee turnover?
* What workforce demographics should HR focus on for retention strategies?

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 👥 Workforce Distribution

| Department             | Employees |
| ---------------------- | --------- |
| Research & Development | 961       |
| Sales                  | 446       |
| Human Resources        | 63        |

* Research & Development accounts for the majority of the workforce.
* Human Resources has the smallest employee population.

### ⏰ Overtime Analysis

| Overtime Status | Employees |
| --------------- | --------- |
| No Overtime     | 1054      |
| Overtime        | 416       |

* Approximately 28% of employees regularly work overtime.
* Overtime is often associated with higher attrition risk and employee burnout.

### 👨‍💼 Gender Distribution

| Gender | Employees |
| ------ | --------- |
| Male   | 882 (60%) |
| Female | 588 (40%) |

* Workforce composition is predominantly male.
* Gender distribution remains relatively balanced across the organization.

### 🎂 Age Group Analysis

| Age Group | Employees |
| --------- | --------- |
| Mid Age   | 679       |
| Senior    | 465       |
| Young     | 326       |

* Mid-age employees represent the largest workforce segment.
* Young employees account for the smallest employee group.

### 💼 Job Role Distribution

| Job Role              | Employees |
| --------------------- | --------- |
| Sales Executive       | 326       |
| Research Scientist    | 292       |
| Laboratory Technician | 259       |

* Sales Executives form the largest job role group.
* Research Scientists and Laboratory Technicians also represent significant workforce segments.

### 🎓 Education Field Analysis

| Education Field | Employees |
| --------------- | --------- |
| Life Sciences   | 606       |
| Medical         | 464       |
| Marketing       | 159       |

* Life Sciences dominates the workforce educational background.
* Medical and Marketing are the next most represented fields.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Monitor employees working overtime and implement workload balancing strategies.
2. Develop targeted retention programs for high-risk employee groups.
3. Improve employee engagement and career development opportunities.
4. Conduct regular satisfaction and performance reviews.
5. Use workforce analytics to proactively identify attrition risks.
